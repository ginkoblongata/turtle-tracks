Differences between Turtle Tracks and UCBLogo

Up: Table of contents

Turtle Tracks was designed to closely match the UCBLogo implementation developed by Brian Harvey at the University of California at Berkeley. Many Logo programs written for UCBLogo will run in Turtle Tracks without modification; however several key differences do exist. This chapter describes some of those differences and how to get around them.
